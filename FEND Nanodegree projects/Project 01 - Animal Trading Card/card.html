Heavy Metal Eddie

Eddie is the mascot for the British heavy metal band Iron Maiden. Eddie features in all of the band's concerts, as well as T-shirts, posters and action figures.

  • Full Name: Eddie the Head
  • Species: Perennial fixture
  • Gender: Male
  • Habitat: Heavy metal tours

Eddie was originally a papier-mâché mask used in Iron Maiden's stage backdrop and did not have a body until their first album 'Iron Maiden' was released in 1980. Eddie is occasionally described as "zombie-like" in the press, but he changes his appearance depending on Maiden's individual albums and their corresponding world tours, and has appeared as a cyborg, an Egyptian mummy and a lobotomised mental patient.
