Swifty Spiderr

Faster than a lightning, sharper than a razor. When he looks into your eyes it can see your soul.

  • Scientific Name: Arachnea Volatilis
  • Average Length: 5cm
  • Average Lifespan: Two centuries
  • Habitat: Underbeds and Incupboards

Swifty Spider has 8 fast and hairy legs that allow him to move very fast. His four eyes can see in the dark and there is no animal nor human who can be missed from his sight. He often hides begind beds of little children or housewifes' pantries.
